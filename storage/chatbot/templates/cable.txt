Cable Modem Trouble Shooting

Please see graphic.

Make sure cable coming in from outside is not damaged.

Turn off power and disconnect CATV connector
(if you have an external wifi router, power that off also).

Wait 2 minutes before connecting the cables again.

Wait 5 minutes for a reboot
Do not power off again.

Receive / Down Light
will blink until connected

Send / Up Light
will blink untill connected

Online / Internet light
solid

Should work now

Send MENU or EXIT
