
DSL Trouble Shooting:

DSL Modem:
Disconnect phone line
Power off
Wait 60 seconds

Connect phone line
Turn on Power
Wait 5 minutes for reboot

DSL light: Solid
Internet Light: blinking

Should work now.

If not, let us know:
  DSL Light:
  SOLID or FLASHING.
Internet Light:
   Blinks or Off

Blinking DSL light means a wire could be disconnected in your house.  You can check that by taking the modem to the NID and testing there for a solid DSL light.

Send MENU or EXIT
